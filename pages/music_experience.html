Currently I teach beginner piano lessons at my residence, and I will teach anyone willing to learn: young, old, brand new, or has starting knowledge. I understand how beneficial music education is, and am also aware that many schools are doing away with it. In response to that, my lessons are very cheap to ensure that anyone can have access to a musical education. If you'd like to inquire more, please see the information on 'Contact Me'.
