The McDaniel Family Potluck for the Mid-Autumn Festival

Saturday 7th October 2023 17:00

What is the Mid-Autumn Festival? The Mid-Autumn Festival, or the Mooncake Festival, is a Chinese holiday celebrated with friends and family, though it is also celebrated in other asian countries. It changes days every year because it is based on the Lunar calendar. There are two main stories surrounding the Mid-Autumn Festival, including the Legend of Change'E and the origin of the mooncake.

There will be some paper activities and a movie ("Over the Moon" starring Philipa Soo) for everyone to enjoy as a break from talking and snacks, which include water and a variety of Chinese teas, as well as some rice noodles and fried rice.
